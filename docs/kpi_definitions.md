# KPI Definitions – International Student Journey Analytics

This document defines key performance indicators (KPIs) used to analyze the international student journey.

---

## 1. Orientation Attendance Rate

**Definition:**
Percentage of students who attended orientation out of those who arrived.

**Formula:**
orientation_attended = Yes / arrival_completed = Yes

**Business Meaning:**
Measures how effectively students are onboarded after arrival.

---

## 2. Engagement Rate

**Definition:**
Percentage of students who participated in at least 1 event.

**Formula:**
event_participation > 0 / total students

**Business Meaning:**
Indicates student involvement and campus engagement.

---

## 3. Retention Rate

**Definition:**
Percentage of students who are retained.

**Formula:**
retained = Yes / total students

**Business Meaning:**
Measures long-term student success and satisfaction.

---

## 4. Document Completion Rate

**Definition:**
Percentage of students who completed check-in after arrival.

**Formula:**
checkin_completed = Yes / arrival_completed = Yes

**Business Meaning:**
Tracks operational efficiency and compliance.

---

## 5. Funnel Drop-off Analysis

**Stages:**
Visa → Arrival → Orientation → Check-in → Engagement → Retention

**Business Meaning:**
Identifies where students drop off in the journey and helps improve those stages.

---

## Why KPIs Matter

These KPIs help universities:

- Improve onboarding experience
- Increase student engagement
- Reduce drop-offs
- Improve retention outcomes
